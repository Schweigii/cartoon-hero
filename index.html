Iron Man

About Iron Man

A rich playboy named Tony Stark, with a huge weapons company, is forced to make himself an iron suit out of scrap metal to free himself from the grip of terorists. Ultimately becoming the one most noticable and famous Marvel superhero, Iron Man.

Iron Mans characteristics

  1. selfish and not a good teamplayer, but with a good heart
  2. can fly and shoot lasers
  3. has a powerful energy source on his chest
  4. just a cool guy
